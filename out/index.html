The VOLT Method

Four
Principles

We built VOLT on the belief that rowing is the most complete full-body workout available — and that most people have never been taught to do it properly.

🚣
Stroke Power

Every stroke is a full-body movement. We coach the drive, catch, finish, and recovery — because form determines output.

📊
Data-Driven

Split times, stroke rate, and watts displayed in real time. VOLT athletes row with intent, not just effort.

🔁
Interval Science

Work-to-rest ratios designed around the rowing stroke. Our programming is built on exercise physiology, not guesswork.

🧠
Mental Edge

Rowing requires sustained focus. Our coaches train the mental side as deliberately as the physical one.

Class Formats

Every Stroke
Counts

Six class formats built for every experience level. Whether you're touching a Concept2 for the first time or chasing a new 2k PR — VOLT has a class for it.

All Levels50 min
Power Row

The flagship VOLT class. Alternating rowing intervals with floor-based strength work. Expect to be challenged from the first pull.

Beginner45 min
Erg 101

Learn proper rowing technique on the Concept2 erg. Stroke mechanics, breathing, and pacing — the foundation for everything at VOLT.

Intermediate45 min
Sprint Series

Short, high-intensity intervals at maximum effort. 20 strokes on, controlled rest. Designed to improve stroke power and anaerobic capacity.

Intermediate+60 min
Endurance Row

Sustained aerobic rowing at a controlled pace. Builds base fitness, improves stroke efficiency, and develops true cardiovascular endurance.

Advanced55 min
Strength + Erg

Compound barbell and dumbbell movements combined with rowing intervals. For athletes who want to build strength alongside endurance.

All Levels30 min
Recovery Row

Low-rate steady-state rowing for active recovery. Keeps the blood moving and maintains stroke technique on rest days.
